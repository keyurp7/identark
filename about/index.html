IdentArk was founded on a simple observation: Indian SMBs were either buying enterprise IAM platforms they couldn't operate, or ignoring identity security entirely.

Our Story

We Exist Between
Two Failure Modes

Indian SMBs were either buying enterprise IAM platforms they couldn't operate, or ignoring identity security entirely and waiting for a breach to force the conversation. IdentArk exists between those two failure modes.

Our team has hands-on delivery experience with SailPoint Identity IQ, Microsoft Entra ID, Okta, and Active Directory across BFSI, healthcare, and technology verticals. We deploy, configure, and hand over working systems — not slide decks.

Founded and operating from India, with deep awareness of the DPDP Act, RBI IT Framework, and SEBI CSCRF requirements that Indian businesses actually face.
